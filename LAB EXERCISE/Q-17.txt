Explanation:-
--------------
Requirement Gathering: Collecting user requirements.

System Design: Planning system architecture and design.

Implementation: Writing the actual code.

Testing: Verifying the functionality.

Deployment: Releasing the software for use.

Maintenance: Ongoing updates and fixes after deployment.
